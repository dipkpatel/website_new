Fun activites and Games to Play

Combines learning with play so that children can integrate sustainable living in a fun way! Here you’ll find games, activities, puzzles, and crafts that you can do with your children. All work and no play makes life dull! Come here to have fun, and maybe learn something at the same time!

Games

Homework is too often no fun, but maybe it can be. These games are intended to let students have fun while learning about sustainable living practices with regards to the 4 curriculum subjects: Waste, Water, Energy and Food. They can be an important additional learning tool to what we learn at the YELL, or they can just be a little bit of fun. Your choice!
