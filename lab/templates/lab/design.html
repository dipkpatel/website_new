Introduction


The design of the Yorklands Eco Learning Lab was created in order to echo the Yorklands Green Hub, colour scheme, both indoors and out. In creating this space, we are using materials that will help us demonstrate different features of the lab, so that it is easier for guests to experience the lab as many ways as possible. We have used innovative materials, features and design elements that will make it accessible to guests, while also making it easy to transport from one site to another. Check out our photo gallery to get a glimpse of our vision for the Yorklands Eco Learning Lab.





Key Features

Core Features

  • Greywater system to capture used water
  • Green Wall
  • Roof with rain water harvesting system
  • UV water treatment system (make water portable)
  • Photovoltaic solar roof panel
  • "Smart" LED lighting
  • Lowatt solid waste management toilet
  • Anaerobic digester to generate power from solid waste


Materials Used


  • Clear PVC pipes
  • Post-consumer cabinets
  • Reclaimed hardwood
  • Sheep wool insulation
  • Natural clay plaster
